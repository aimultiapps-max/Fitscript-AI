# Application Usage Guide

Welcome to FitScript AI.

## 1. Getting started

- Sign in with Google, Apple, or continue as a guest.
- Choose your preferred app language from the available menu.

## 2. Upload your lab document

- Use camera, gallery, or PDF document upload.
- Make sure the selected file is clear and readable.

## 3. Analyze and save results

- Tap the analyze button to process your lab result.
- Save analysis results to review history and progress.

## 4. Account and synchronization

- Connect a Google/Apple account to sync your data across devices.
- Guest mode stores data locally on the current device.

## 5. Important note

FitScript AI is an educational and informational assistant. App output is not a substitute for professional medical diagnosis or advice.

## 6. Support contact

If you experience issues while using the app, contact the development team through the official support channel.
